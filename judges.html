Eligibility Requirements

Junior Division

Grades 6–8  ·  21 categories

  • check_circle Certified science, engineering, math, or CS teachers (cannot judge their own school's students)
  • check_circle Individuals holding degrees in STEM fields
  • check_circle STEM professionals
  • check_circle Undergraduate pre-service teachers or STEM degree candidates (Juniors/Seniors with research experience)
  • check_circle Retired individuals meeting the above criteria

Senior Division

High School  ·  15 categories

  • check_circle STEM degree holders, preferably with college poster session or science fair experience
  • check_circle Graduate STEM degree students
  • check_circle High school STEM teachers (cannot judge their own school's students)
  • check_circle STEM professionals with five or more years of experience
  • check_circle Retired individuals meeting the above criteria

How to Register

  1. 1

    Create an account

    Access the online registration system at app.lascifair.org and enter your information.

  2. 2

    Select your division and categories

    Indicate your preferred division (Junior or Senior) and up to three project categories you are qualified to evaluate.

  3. 3

    Questions? Contact us

    For additional preferences or questions, email judging@lascifair.org.
