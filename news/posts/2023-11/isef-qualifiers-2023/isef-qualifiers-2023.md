## What Is ISEF?

The International Science and Engineering Fair is the world's largest pre-college science competition, bringing together roughly 1,800 students from 70+ countries. Top prizes include $75,000 scholarships and the opportunity to have a minor planet named after you.

## How LACSEF Students Are Selected

The two Grand Award winners in each division automatically qualify. Additional bids are awarded by category based on ISEF's allocation to LA County. Category winners who are not Grand Award recipients are ranked by judge score to fill remaining spots.
