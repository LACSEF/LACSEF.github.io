LACSEF has finalized partnerships with **10 new schools** for the 2024 fair cycle, expanding our reach into communities that have historically been underrepresented in county-level science competitions.

## New Partner Schools

**Antelope Valley Region**

- Palmdale Learning Plaza
- Desert Winds Continuation High School
- Quartz Hill High School

**South Bay Region**

- Gardena High School
- Narbonne High School
- Leuzinger High School

**Southeast LA Region**

- Bell Gardens High School
- Cudahy Middle School
- Lynwood High School
- South Gate High School

## What Partnership Means

Partner schools receive:

- Priority access to mentor matching
- A dedicated LACSEF liaison who visits once per semester
- Discounted (or free) science fair supplies through our materials-grant program
- Professional development workshop for up to three teachers per school

## Why This Matters

Last year, just 12% of our participants came from schools in the bottom quartile of LA County per-pupil spending, despite those schools enrolling 38% of the county's students. Closing that gap is a multi-year effort — and it starts with showing up.

If your school is not on this list but is interested in partnering for 2025, contact partnerships@lacsef.org.
