All 16 competition categories for the 2024 fair are now finalized. Choosing the right category is one of the most important decisions you will make — a mismatched category can disadvantage even a strong project.

## How to Choose

Ask yourself: _What is the primary question my project answers?_ Match it to the category that best reflects that question, not the materials you used. A project that uses chemicals to study plant growth belongs in Plant Sciences, not Chemistry.
