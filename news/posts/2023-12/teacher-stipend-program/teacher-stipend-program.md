For the third consecutive year, LACSEF is offering a **$500 classroom stipend** to teachers who actively sponsor student projects. Funding is limited to 30 educators and awarded on a first-come, first-served basis.

## Eligibility

- You must be a credentialed teacher at an LA County school (public, private, or charter)
- You must sponsor at least **three registered student projects** in the 2024 fair
- Projects must be submitted before the February 15 deadline
- You must submit a brief post-fair report (one page) on how the stipend was used

## What the Stipend Covers

The $500 is unrestricted classroom funding. Past recipients have used it for lab supplies, reference books, student printing costs, and field trips related to their projects. Receipts are not required, but funds must be used for educational purposes.

## Application Timeline

| Date        | Milestone            |
| ----------- | -------------------- |
| January 15  | Applications open    |
| February 20 | Application deadline |
| March 1     | Award notifications  |
| April 15    | Stipend disbursed    |
| May 15      | Post-fair report due |

## How to Apply

Log in to the Teacher Portal and select **Stipend Program 2024**. The application takes about 10 minutes. You will need your school's district code, which is available from your school office.

Questions? Email teachers@lacsef.org.
