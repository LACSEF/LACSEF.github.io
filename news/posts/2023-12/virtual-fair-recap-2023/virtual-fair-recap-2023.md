The 2023 LA County Science & Engineering Fair brought together **412 projects** from 180 schools across all 80 school districts in LA County. Here are the highlights.

## By the Numbers

- 412 projects across 15 categories
- 180 participating schools
- 238 judges from 60 organizations
- 14 projects earned bids to the California State Science Fair
- 6 projects earned bids to ISEF

## Grand Award Winners

**Senior Division**

- _"Machine Learning Detection of Early-Stage Diabetic Retinopathy from Smartphone Fundus Images"_ — Yuna Park, Arcadia High School

**Junior Division**

- _"Quantifying Microplastic Accumulation in Three LA River Tributary Sediments"_ — Elias Ruiz, Gage Middle School

## Special Awards

Over 30 organizations presented special awards totaling more than $45,000 in scholarships and prizes. The full award list is available on the Results page.

## What We Heard from Judges

The feedback from this year's judging panel highlighted an overall improvement in experimental design quality compared to 2022, with particular strength in the Earth & Environmental Sciences and Behavioral & Social Sciences categories. The most consistent critique: abstracts that bury the results.

## Looking Ahead to 2024

Registration for the 2024 fair is now open. We will be adding a Data Science & AI category, expanding mentor program capacity, and partnering with 10 new schools in underserved regions. See you in March.
