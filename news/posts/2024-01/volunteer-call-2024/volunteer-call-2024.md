Fair Day only works because hundreds of volunteers show up. We need **82 volunteers** across four shifts on March 22–23, and you do not need a science background to help.

## What Volunteers Receive

- Official LACSEF volunteer badge
- Lunch provided on both days
- A letter of service for college or community-service records

## How to Sign Up

Visit the Volunteer Portal and select your preferred shift. Spots fill quickly — the awards-setup role sold out in three days last year. Sign up by February 20 to guarantee your preferred shift.

Questions? Email volunteers@lacsef.org or call the main office Tuesday–Thursday, 9 AM–3 PM.
