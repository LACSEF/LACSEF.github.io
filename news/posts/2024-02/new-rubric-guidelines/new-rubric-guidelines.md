The judging committee has spent the off-season updating evaluation rubrics for the Senior Division engineering categories. The revised rubrics better reflect modern prototyping standards.

## What's Changed

Three rubric criteria received the most significant updates:

1. **Engineering Process** — now weights iteration and failure documentation more heavily
2. **Technical Depth** — explicitly recognizes use of modern simulation and ML tools
3. **Communication** — added scoring for accessible language and visual storytelling

Judges should review the updated rubrics in the Judge Portal before fair day.
