Safety is not a formality — it is a core part of scientific practice. LACSEF requires all projects involving the items below to receive **written approval before any experimentation begins**.

## Pre-Experiment Checklist

Before you run your first trial, confirm all of the following:

- [ ] You have read the [Student Safety Guidelines](#) in full
- [ ] Your supervising teacher has signed the Safety Acknowledgment Form
- [ ] Hazardous chemicals are stored and disposed of per SDS instructions
- [ ] Any human subjects research has IRB or school-equivalent approval
- [ ] Vertebrate animal studies have IACUC approval
- [ ] All electrical components are rated for your power source

## Common Approval Mistakes

**Skipping the pre-approval form** is the leading cause of project disqualification. The form must be submitted before experimentation, not at registration time.

**"Low risk" is not an exception.** Even projects using only household materials must complete the checklist — judges will ask.

## Where to Get Approval

Submit the Research Plan & Safety Form through the Student Portal. Allow **10 school days** for review. If you need expedited review, email safety@lacsef.org with your project title and proposed start date.
