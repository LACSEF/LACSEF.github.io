We are **thrilled** to announce that registration for the 74th Annual LA County Science & Engineering Fair is now officially open. Students, teachers, and judges can begin the onboarding process today.

## Key Dates

- **Registration deadline:** December 1, 2024
- **Project submission:** February 15, 2025
- **Fair day:** March 22-23, 2025

## Who Can Participate

Students in grades 6-12 enrolled at any LA County school are eligible. Each project must be sponsored by a teacher and adhere to our safety guidelines.

> "The Science Fair has consistently been the proving ground for the next generation of researchers and engineers." — Dr. Aris Thorne, Chief Judge
