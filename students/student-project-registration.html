Student Project Registration

All participants in the Annual Los Angeles County Science & Engineering Fair must complete online registration for both themselves and their projects — including students who have already received pre-approval.

info

Overview

The Fair President reviews each submitted project to determine admission eligibility. Students receive email notifications regarding acceptance or rejection decisions — make sure to provide a valid email address that can receive external messages.

Accepted students will receive a Final Information Packet via email several weeks before the event.

warning

Projects Requiring Pre-Approval

Certain project types require mandatory pre-approval from the Science Review Committee before experimentation begins. Projects lacking required approval will be rejected from participation.

  • check_circle Projects involving human subjects
  • check_circle Projects using vertebrate animals or higher-functioning invertebrates
  • check_circle Projects involving Potentially Hazardous Biological Agents (PHBA)
  • check_circle Projects containing Hazardous Chemicals, Activities, or Devices (HCAD)
