Understanding SRC Results

As the Science Review Committee (SRC) evaluates projects, here's how to read each status in the online registration dashboard and what to do next.

fact_check

Status Indicators

hourglass_empty

Awaiting Verification

One or more supervising adults haven't confirmed their role. Check email (including spam) for messages from noreply@lascifair.org. If links have expired or weren't received, contact president@lascifair.org.

hourglass_top

Awaiting Level 1 Review

All verifications are complete. Your project is in the queue for the first SRC reviewer.

rate_review

Level 1 Recommended Revision

The first reviewer flagged concerns. The project automatically advances to a second reviewer — no action needed yet.

thumb_up

Level 1 Recommended Acceptance

The first reviewer approved. The project advances to a second reviewer for confirmation.

hourglass_top

Awaiting Level 2 Review

Your project is pending assessment from the second reviewer.

edit_note

Needs Revision

The second reviewer rejected the proposal. You have one opportunity to address all comments from both reviewers and resubmit before the deadline. Experimentation cannot begin without approval.

check_circle

Accepted

Your proposal has been approved. You may now commence experimentation.

gavel

Awaiting Level 3 Review

The second reviewer requested a third-party evaluation. That reviewer's decision is final.

refresh

Resubmission & Post-Approval Changes

After approval, editing is disabled. If you need to make changes to an approved project, email pre-approval@lascifair.org with a full explanation.

Resubmitted projects follow the same review procedure. Approval allows experimentation to proceed.

All decisions by the SRC Committee are final.
