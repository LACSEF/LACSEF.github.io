Volunteer at LACSEF

Volunteers are the backbone of the fair. From setting up displays to guiding students through check-in, your 2.5-hour shift makes the day run smoothly for hundreds of young scientists.

Shift Length

2.5 Hours

School Volunteer Quotas

Each participating school is expected to provide adult volunteers in proportion to the number of projects it submits. Recruiting from your school community is a great way to fill your quota.

looks_one

1–6 Projects

1 adult volunteer required from your school.

looks_two

7–13 Projects

2 adult volunteers required from your school.

how_to_reg

Judge Credit

A school that provides a judge receives credit for 2 volunteers toward its quota.

app_registration

How to Register

  1. 1

    Create an account

    Go to app.lascifair.org and register with your information. You will receive an automated password by email within 72 hours.

  2. 2

    Select your shift(s)

    Log back in once you receive your password and choose your preferred volunteer positions, dates, and times.

  3. 3

    Already registered as a judge or site coordinator?

    Use a different email address to create a separate volunteer account.
